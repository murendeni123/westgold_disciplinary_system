EXCEL IMPORT HEADERS FOR WESTGOLD DISCIPLINARY SYSTEM
=====================================================

STUDENTS IMPORT
---------------
Required: student_id, first_name, last_name, class_name, date_of_birth, gender
Optional: grade_level, parent_email, parent_name, parent_phone, medical_info

Example:
student_id | first_name | last_name | class_name | date_of_birth | gender | grade_level | parent_email
2024001    | John       | Smith     | 5A         | 2014-05-15    | Male   | 5           | parent@email.com


TEACHERS IMPORT
---------------
Required: name, email
Optional: phone, employee_id, department, subjects, is_admin

Example:
name         | email                    | phone      | department   | subjects
John Teacher | john.teacher@school.com  | 0821234567 | Mathematics  | Math, Science


PARENTS IMPORT
--------------
Required: name, email
Optional: phone, relationship_to_child, home_address, city, postal_code

Example:
name        | email                | phone      | relationship_to_child
Jane Smith  | jane.smith@email.com | 0821234567 | Mother


CLASSES IMPORT
--------------
Required: class_name, grade_level
Optional: teacher_email, academic_year

Example:
class_name | grade_level | teacher_email           | academic_year
5A         | 5           | teacher@school.com      | 2024


ATTENDANCE IMPORT
-----------------
Required: student_id, date, status
Optional: notes

Example:
student_id | date       | status  | notes
2024001    | 2024-01-25 | present |
2024002    | 2024-01-25 | absent  | Sick


BEHAVIOUR INCIDENTS
-------------------
Required: student_id, teacher_email, description, date
Optional: incident_type, severity, points_deducted

Example:
student_id | teacher_email          | description      | date       | points_deducted
2024001    | teacher@school.com     | Disrupting class | 2024-01-25 | 5


MERITS
------
Required: student_id, teacher_email, description, points, date
Optional: merit_type

Example:
student_id | teacher_email      | description        | points | date
2024001    | teacher@school.com | Excellent homework | 5      | 2024-01-25


IMPORTANT NOTES:
- Headers are case-sensitive and must match exactly
- Use YYYY-MM-DD format for dates
- First row must be headers
- No empty rows between headers and data
- Save as .xlsx or .csv format
